01

Inquiry

Start your journey. Get in touch to discuss your vision. Whether you have a clear concept or just a feeling you want to capture, every commission begins with a conversation.

  • Share reference photos, mood boards, or conceptual ideas
  • Discuss timeline, size preferences, and intended display space
  • Receive preliminary guidance on style direction and feasibility
  • No obligation — explore possibilities before committing
02

Consultation

A personal dialog to refine your style palette, composition, and dimensions. This is where your vision takes shape through collaborative exploration. We'll discuss every detail that will make your portrait uniquely yours.

  • Video call or messaging — typically 30-45 minutes to explore your vision in depth
  • Style direction — classical, contemporary, surreal, or a unique fusion tailored to your taste
  • Color palette selection — warm tones, cool atmospheres, vibrant expressions, or muted elegance
  • Composition planning — framing, background elements, symbolic details, and focal points
  • Size and material recommendations — optimized for your space and display preferences
  • Outcome: A detailed creative brief with style directions, palette swatches, and composition sketches

What to prepare: Reference images, color preferences, and any symbolic elements you'd like incorporated.

03

Creation

Crafted pixel by pixel in the Haarlem studio. This is where the magic happens — your portrait comes to life through meticulous digital craftsmanship, blending classical artistry with modern precision.

Draft

Initial composition and structure established based on our consultation

Build

Layer by layer, details emerge — skin tones, textures, light, and shadow

Internal QA

Rigorous review for color accuracy, composition balance, and emotional impact

Private Preview

Your first look at the completed artwork, ready for your feedback

  • Regular progress updates with work-in-progress previews
  • Multiple refinement passes to achieve the perfect expression
  • Color-calibrated workflow ensuring print accuracy
04

Review & Refinement

Private preview with up to two revision rounds until perfection. This collaborative phase ensures the final artwork exceeds your expectations and captures exactly what you envisioned.

  • Private preview — High-resolution watermarked image for your review
  • Up to two revision rounds included with every commission
  • What counts as a revision: Color adjustments, minor compositional changes, detail refinements
  • What does not count: Complete style changes or new concepts (these require a new commission)
  • Typical turnaround: 3-5 business days per revision round
05

Delivery

Your final masterpiece, delivered as a museum-grade print or high-res digital file. We ensure your artwork arrives ready to become a cherished centerpiece in your home or collection.

  • High-resolution digital file — Print-ready at your specified dimensions
  • Museum-grade print options — Canvas, aluminum, acrylic glass, or ArtFrame
  • Print purchases via Werk aan de Muur — Professional fulfillment with free shipping
  • Shipping notifications — Track your print from production to delivery
  • Support: For print-related inquiries, contact Werk aan de Muur directly; for artwork questions, reach out to floris@oever.art

Note: Production and fulfillment for print orders are handled by Werk aan de Muur, a trusted print-on-demand platform. OEVER.ART is an independent creator.

The Artist

Floris van de
Oever

From a childhood spent surrounded by his grandparents' art collection near Artis in Amsterdam, Floris developed an obsessive eye for detail and a deep love for portraiture.

Today, working from his studio in Haarlem, he merges classical composition with digital precision — creating hyper-realistic portraits where every pixel, every color choice, and every shadow serves the emotional truth of the subject.

His work explores the themes of feminine beauty, nature, and the interplay between the organic and the digital — always pursuing perfection, never quite satisfied, always pushing further.
